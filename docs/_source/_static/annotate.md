####  Gene annotations and taxonomy annotations 
---

You can use [pharokka](https://github.com/gbouras13/pharokka) or [phage_contig_annotator](https://github.com/Yasas1994/phage_contig_annotator) to annotate and visualize Jaeger predictions.

For taxonomy annotations you can use [VCAT](https://github.com/Yasas1994/vcat).
